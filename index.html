Delete Your Paisa Manager Account

At Paisa Manager, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal data. You can permanently delete your account directly from within the app at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

Step 1: Open the Paisa Manager app.
Step 2: Sign in to your account.
Step 3: Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
Step 4: Confirm your password (or Google account if applicable).
Step 5: Tap Delete Account.
Step 6: Your account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • Your Firebase Authentication account
  • Your profile information
  • Your expense records
  • Your income records
  • Your budgets
  • Your savings goals
  • Your reminders and notifications
  • Your AI Financial Coach history (if stored)
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Data Retention

After your deletion request is completed, your personal data is permanently removed from our systems unless we are legally required to retain specific information for compliance with applicable laws.
